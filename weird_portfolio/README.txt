WEIRD — Portfolio Website
=========================

Files:
  index.html         — Main portfolio page
  odessa-tour.html   — Case Study 01: Odessa Tour
  guitar-shop.html   — Case Study 02: Guitar Shop

To use:
  1. Open index.html in your browser to preview
  2. Replace photo placeholder: find <img id="profile-photo"> in index.html
     and change the src to your photo file (place photo in same folder)
     e.g. src="photo.jpg"
  3. To deploy: upload the entire folder to Netlify (drag & drop, free)
