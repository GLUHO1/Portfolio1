About

Design is not about beauty.
It's about clarity.

I approach UX as a research practice: first I understand, then I design. A background in psychology helps me see behavioral patterns rather than just moving elements around a screen.

What fascinates me is the moment when an interface disappears — when a person simply does what they came to do without ever noticing the design. That's the goal.
